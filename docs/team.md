## Katsumata Masashi – Lead Developer & Founder

Masashi is a Google Developer Expert (GDE) for Maps with around a decade of experience building map‑based applications and tools. He has authored several books on Google Maps APIs and created the Cordova Google Maps Plugin, which has been widely used by developers around the world. His experience with different map SDKs and real projects is the basis for MapConductor’s unified design.

## Takaaki Omori – Software Engineer / Co‑maintainer

Takaaki contributes to the core development of MapConductor and helps maintain the codebase as the project evolves.
